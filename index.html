Haikyuu!!!

A Volleyball Journey.







Konnichiwa.

Haikyuu is the one of the best anime's out there related to sports. It focuses on volleyball and all the obstacles that 'Karasuno High School' faces while trying to make a name for themselves at various tournaments.



More about Karasuno High's Volleyball Team:

The Coach:

Coach Ukai is the current coach of Karasuno High School's Boys Volleyball team. He was also an alumnus and former player on the team, playing as a setter. As a coach, Ukai has very high abilities in analysis and tactics. He goes the extra distance to collect as much data on their opponents as possible in order to formulate an effective plan to counteract them.

The Manager:

The former manager of the boys volleyball team, Kiyoko Shimizu, was previously a member of her middle school's track and field team who specialized in hurdles. In her first year of high school, she was recruited by Daichi Sawamura to become the manager for Karasuno's Boys' Volleyball Club. Hitoka Yachi was recruited by Kiyoko as her replacement for when she graduates. Despite being intimidated by them, she quickly grew to like the volleyball team.
